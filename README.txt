This assessment was an Academic Survey paper conducted on the current state of Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) and a look to how it can be scaled up in the future to wide scale use. 

The paper was written as a two person group with authors Joel Ansbro and Daniel Smith (now Daniel Loddington). 

A presentation on the paper can be found here https://youtu.be/fiVEAY0jHY8 and was prepared by Daniel Loddington and recorded and performed by Joel Ansbro.
